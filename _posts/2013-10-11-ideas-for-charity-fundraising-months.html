Prompted by Howard Lake’s collection of charity campaigns that hijack month names, here is a year’s worth of month-punning ideas that any charity is free to take and run with. I hereby relinquish all claim on these names and concepts. You don’t even have to credit me. You’re welcome.

Fanuary

Concept: Support (wholeheartedly) your rival football team, sing their songs, cheer their goals
Suitable causes: men’s health

FABruary

Concept: Walk like one of the Thunderbirds puppets (it can be done)
Suitable causes: international rescue

Merch March

Concept: Sell as much charity tat (pens, mugs, etc) as you can, run a league table with rewards for the winners
Suitable causes: all

Staypril

Concept: Shop local – no supermarkets, no online shopping
Suitable causes: local community

May Be Baby

Concept: Dress up in one of those baby costumes (WARNING: link is a bit creepy)
Suitable causes: infant health

June Bug

Concept: Do something to encourage insect life
Suitable causes: wildlife

Jewly

Concept: Go kosher for a month
Suitable causes: Jewish

Drawgust

Concept: Cause beneficiaries and/or supporters draw something every day to illustrate the cause, best ones exhibited / auctioned off
Suitable for: any human cause

Schleptember

Concept: Walk everywhere, no cars, no public transport
Suitable causes: health

Rocktober

Concept: Perform air guitar in public every day for a month
Suitable causes: hearing

Yes/No-vember

Concept: Base all your decisions on the toss of a coin
Suitable causes: gambling

Debtcember

Concept: Live by begging and borrowing (but not stealing)
Suitable causes: homelessness
